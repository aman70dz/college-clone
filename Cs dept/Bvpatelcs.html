Welcome to Bhulabhai Vanmalibhai Patel Institute of Computer Science

Founded in 1998

We are currently offering 3-year graduate professional programms in Bechalor of Computer Application (BCA). We are constituent of Uka Tarsadia University, Bardoli. Starting with modest strength of 60 students in 1998, the Institute has progressed by leaps and bounds over the years and with the student strength of around 600, it has emerged as a brand in itself in the South Gujarat region. Initially meant to cater the students of Bardoli and nearby areas, the Institute has become a favoured destination for students across the state.

The institute offers best in terms of infrastructure with ergonomically designed auditorium, seminar hall and classrooms; all equipped with computer and modern teaching aids. State of the art computer facility with 50+ servers, more than 300 computers and printers networked with Wi-Fi Technology, to be rigorously used by the students and faculty alike in order to derive maximum learning experience.

Our curricula offer a wide range of exciting electives and core courses. Here 3-D learning environment of dialogue, discussion and discourse groom students for life. Unique blend of theories with field experiences, industrial visits, expert talks, enough occasions of fun and frolic coupled with nurturing of health and soul quotients provides a holistic education. Meticulously planned teaching schedule and calendar of activities create unbounded opportunities to express leadership, creativity and other multiple talents. The robust involvement of professionally qualified faculties and attention to details with ample human quotient enable the newbies to rise and move ahead with acquired competencies and confidence. Our academic programmes act as a gateway for higher education in India and abroad. That is, we open the path to move from local roots to global routes.

Source of Inspiration

Shri Bhulabhai Vanmalibhai Patel

The Institute owes its existence to the inspiration of renowned philanthropist Shri Bhulabhai Vanmalibhai Patel, fondly known as Bhula
Dada among students and staff of the Institute. Shri Bhula Dada, who was an astute and highly successful businessperson in the USA,
came back to his village and started philanthropic activities. Till date, he has patronized over 40 organizations ranging from education to
health care to welfare trusts. Bhula Dada has showered his love and affection on our Institute. Not only does he help us on a regular
basis, but he also ensures that we remain committed to our goal of providing holistic education with emphasis on value education.
